About Me :

Hi! I'm Michelle, a photographer based in Bellevue, Washington. I'm constantly looking for the decisive moment that captures your *spark*, tells your unique story, and preserves your special moments, milestones, and life events. Whether it's a senior photo, event, or family portrait, this time in your life deserves to be preserved, remembered, and cherished.

Portraits/Families:

You may have heard the phrase, "The days are long, but the years are short." If you are here, you already know how true that statement is. Preserve each season of your life with photos that capture joyful, authentic, and timeless memories. As your photographer, I strive to catch the moment, optimize the light, enhance the truth, and develop the clarity while exercising patience and kindness.

Events:

Taking on a more documentary style, I capture lifestyle and events with a high regard for joyful, authentic, and timeless imagery. I look at each client's project as the next blank canvas, and every project, every job is my next work of art. Let me preserve and protect the memories of your special event or tell your unique brand story.
